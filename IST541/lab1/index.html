How to Make a Potato Frittata

By Kristin Wolf

Step 1: Gather your ingredients

You will need:

    6 eggs
    2 medium potatoes, diced
    1/2 onion, diced
    4 cloves garlic, sliced
    1/2 cup shredded cheese (or slightly more, your preference)
    2 Tbsps olive oil

    1 tsp. salt & 1/4 tsp. pepper

Optional: 1/2 cup addt'l chopped veggies or greens
Step 2: Prepare the vegetables for saute

Chop the onions and garlic, set aside.

Chop potatos into cubes (1/2 inch, approx).

If adding additional vegetables, prepare them to be added at appropriate time, during saute. (Add peppers with potatoes, add chopped greens during last few mintues of saute.

Step 3: Prepare the eggs

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (you'll be baking the eggs).

Prepare a 13x9 inch baking pan by coating it with non-stick spray or olive oil.

Crack the 6 eggs into a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Wisk just until blended and set aside.

Step 4: Sautee the vegetables

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and season with salt, pepper, and spices. Cook, stirring occasionaly, until onions are soft. Add garlic and stir. Add potatos, stirring occasionally, until done (approx. 12 minutes). If adding other veggies to saute, incorporate them according to cooking time. Add addt'l olive oil, as necessary, to keep veggies from sticking to pan.

Step 5: Layer all ingredients into a baking dish

Place the baking dish on a level surface and transfer the sauteed vegetables to the baking dish. Spread the veggies evenly across the bottom of the dish.

Pour the wisked eggs evenly over vegetables. Use a spoon to gently coat all vegetables with egg.

Now, add the shredded cheese to the top of the egg and vegetable mixture as a final layer. You can add a bit more than the 1/2 cup of cheese called for in the recipe if you prefer.

Step 6: Bake and enjoy!

Place the pan in the center of the preheated oven (400 degrees F), and cook for 10-12 minutes. The cheese will be melted, but allowing the cheese to brown may lead to overcooking the eggs. Remove the pan from the oven when the eggs are cooked through at the center. Allow the dish to cool for a a couple of minutes prior to slicing and serving.

Serve with your favorite hot sauce, sour cream, or condiments. Consider including sides such as fresh greens, sliced tomatoes or fruit to make it a meal. Enjoy!
