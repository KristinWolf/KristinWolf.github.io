How to Make a Potato Frittata

By Kristin Wolf

Step 1: Gather your ingredients

To make 4-6 servings, you will need:

    6 eggs
    2 medium potatoes, diced
    1/2 onion, diced
    4 cloves garlic, sliced
    1/2 cup shredded cheese (your preference)
    2 Tbsps olive oil

    Salt & pepper to taste (plus, add your own favorite seasonings)

Optional: add 1/2 cup chopped vegetables such as Swiss chard, tomatoes, broccoli, or peppers .

Step 2: Prepare the vegetables

Chop the onions and garlic, set aside.

Chop potatos into cubes (1/2 inch, approx).

If adding additional vegetables, prepare them in advance.

Step 3: Prepare the eggs

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Farenheit (you'll be baking the eggs).

Prepare a 13x9 inch baking pan by coating with non-stick spray or olive oil.

Crack the 6 eggs into a bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Wisk just til blended and set aside.

Step 4: Sautee the vegetables

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and season with salt, pepper, and spices. Cook, stirring occasionaly, until onions are soft. Add garlic and stir. Add potatos, stirring occasionally, until done (approx. 12 minutes). If incorporating other veggies, add according to cooking time. Add small amounts of olive oil if necessary to keep food from sticking to pan.

Step 5: Layer the ingredients in a baking dish

Use a spoon to transfer the sauteed vegetables to the baking dish. Spread the vegetables evenly over the bottom of the dish.

Pour the wisked eggs evenly over vegetables. Use a spoon to gently coat all vegetables with egg.

Now, add the shredded cheese to the top of the egg and vegetable mixture as a final layer.

Step 6: Bake and enjoy!

Place the pan in the center of the preheated oven (400 degrees F), and cook for 10-12 minutes. Remove when cheese has melted. Allow the dish to cool for a few minutes before serving with your favorite hot sauce, sour cream, and condiments, and sides liked fresh greens or sliced fruit.

Frittata is a protein-rich, comfort food that can be enjoyed at any time of day. Refrigerated leftovers will stay fresh for 2-3 days, and can be heated in a microwave oven. Enjoy!
